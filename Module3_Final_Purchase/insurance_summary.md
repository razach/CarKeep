## 2. Guestimate: 2024 BMW iX xDrive50
When you call your agent (Casey Whitmarsh: 703-425-2828), prepare for a noticeable jump in premium. 

**Why it will be more expensive:**
1. **Replacement Value:** The insurance company looks at the original $97,000 liability replacement cost of the vehicle, not just the $50k you are paying for it.
2. **EV Repair Costs:** BMW's "Carbon Core" frame and the high-voltage battery array are notoriously expensive to repair in the event of a collision compared to your Acura. Structural damage typically totals the car.
3. **Performance:** With over 500 horsepower, the iX falls into a higher risk category for underwriters.

**The Projection:**
In the financial model we ran earlier this week, we safely padded a **~$1,000/year premium increase** into your Total Cost of Ownership. 

* **Current RDX Target:** ~$744 / year
* **Expected iX Surcharge:** +$800 to +$1,200 / year
* **Estimated Target for BMW iX:** **~$1,550 to ~$1,950 / year** *(~$130 to ~$160/mo)*

**Tips for your call:** 
Make sure your agent transfers your "Drive Safe & Save" beacon and your "Multiple Line" (home/renters) discounts to the new quote. Also, you may want to increase your Property Damage limit from $25,000 to $50,000 or $100,000. If you are at fault in an accident with another luxury EV, $25,000 will not cover their car, leaving you personally liable for the difference!
