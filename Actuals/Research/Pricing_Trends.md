### Key Observations:
*   **Depreciation Curve:** There is a significant price drop from the 2024 and 2025 models down to the 2022-2023 models, with the average price falling by approximately **$9,400 to $23,000** respectively.
*   **Entry Point:** The lowest entry price for a BMW iX with Driving Assistance is currently **$35,990** (2022-2023 models), while the newest 2026 models start at **$66,995**.
*   **Market Volume:** The 2024 and 2025 models currently have the highest volume of available listings, suggesting a robust secondary market for these slightly newer vehicles.
*   **Pricing Stability:** The 2026 models show a very tight range around the $88,000 mark, indicating they are likely new or very near-new inventory.
