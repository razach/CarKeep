# Used Auto Loan Rates (Early 2026)

Based on a web search for individuals with excellent credit seeking a 60-month used car auto loan in early 2026:

*   **Average Rates:** Typically range from **4.79% to 5.59% APR**.
*   **Specific Lenders:**
    *   **Navy Federal Credit Union:** As low as 4.79% for 37-60 months.
    *   **Capital One:** Starting at 5.59% for 60 months.
    *   **PenFed:** Promotional rates around 3.84% (assuming excellent credit).
*   **Historical Context:** In 2024, the average used car loan rate for excellent credit was higher, around 6.82%.
